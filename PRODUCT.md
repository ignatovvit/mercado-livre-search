# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People searching Mercado Livre Brazil who need to compare many products quickly without manually opening dozens of tabs. The primary user can search in Russian or English and needs the extension to map terms into pt-BR, scan product pages, and surface trustworthy matches.

## Product Purpose

The product is a Chrome extension for enhanced Mercado Livre product search. It adds a right-side assistant panel on `mercadolivre.com.br`, scans listings and product detail pages until the user stops it, filters by rating, review count, title, description, review text, seller reputation, and material, then lets the user highlight, hide, reorder, or reset page results.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, premium.

The interface should feel like a modern Apple-grade utility: high trust, low noise, strong alignment, readable dense controls, and glass used as purposeful material rather than decoration.

## Anti-references

Avoid primitive dashboard mockups, generic SaaS cards, heavy purple gradients, low-contrast gray text, decorative glassmorphism, misaligned form fields, arbitrary spacing, and cluttered marketplace panels that look bolted onto the page.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the product search task in focus: filters, scan progress, and ranked results must be faster to parse than Mercado Livre's native UI.
2. Use glass to separate extension controls from page content, not to obscure readability.
3. Make dense controls feel deliberate through consistent 8px spacing, aligned fields, and stable component sizing.
4. Treat translation and dictionary matching as first-class workflow state, visible but not noisy.
5. Keep all page actions reversible: highlight, hide, reorder, and reset must feel controlled.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and essential UI. Do not rely on color alone for status. Preserve visible focus states, clear labels, reduced-motion compatibility, and readable text on translucent surfaces.
